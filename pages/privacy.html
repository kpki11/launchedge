Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026  ·  Last updated: June 2026  ·  LaunchEdge, India

At LaunchEdge, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We keep it simple and honest because we believe you deserve to know exactly what happens with your data.

1. Who We Are

LaunchEdge is a business productivity platform built for Indian businesses. We are registered in India (2026). Our website is launchedge.in and our app is LaunchEdge Labs (Android).

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect what is necessary:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, and message when you write to us via the website.
  • Feedback submissions — any feedback or comments you voluntarily submit through our feedback form.
  • Analytics — anonymous visitor counts (pages visited, country, browser type). We do not use cookies or track you personally for analytics.
  • App usage — if you use the LaunchEdge Labs app, we may collect basic usage data (feature usage, crash reports) to improve the product. No personal data is sold or shared.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, passwords, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your messages and feedback
  • To improve our product and fix issues
  • To understand how people use our website (anonymous analytics only)
  • To send product updates if you have opted in (you can opt out any time)

We will never sell your data to anyone. We will never share it with advertisers. We do not use it for any purpose other than what is listed above.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Formspree — processes contact/feedback form submissions and forwards them to our email. See their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • GoatCounter — anonymous website analytics. No cookies, no personal tracking. See goatcounter.com/help/privacy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your contact form messages are stored in our email inbox and are not shared with third parties. We use standard security practices to protect this data. As an early-stage product, we are continuously improving our security posture.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at the address below.

7. Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of India, including applicable provisions of the Information Technology Act, 2000 and rules made thereunder.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product grows. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top. If there are significant changes, we will notify users through the website.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out:

Email: privacy@launchedge.in

Contact form: launchedge.in/contact
