Our Story

Built for Bharat.
By Bharat.

LaunchEdge is the operating system for modern Indian businesses — a single intelligent platform where you can design, automate, hire and grow, all in one place.

Why we built this

India has millions of small businesses, startups, and entrepreneurs who are building something real. But the tools they use are either too expensive, too complex, or not built for the way Indian businesses actually work.

We built LaunchEdge to change that. A platform that understands GST, UPI, local workflows, and the unique challenges of building a business in India — without costing a fortune.

We registered the business in 2026 and shipped the first version within months. We are early, we are honest about it, and we are building fast.

Where we are going

LaunchEdge is currently in Version 1 — with the core Labs app available as a direct download, CoPilot and Talent on the way, and a growing ecosystem of tools designed around Indian business needs.

Our roadmap includes a full Play Store release, deeper AI capabilities through CoPilot, a talent marketplace connecting MSMEs with professionals across India, and much more.

We are building in public, listening to feedback, and improving every week. If you have an idea or want to collaborate, we would love to hear from you.
